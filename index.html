SHOOTING TIPS

Hand Position

  • Angle between your handof your elbow should be free and straight
  • head must be straight and you must look at the ring
  • hold the ball along its grooves for better grip and concentration
  • release the ball with a spin aiming towards the back of the ring
  • keep your follow-through after the shot for a few seconds

Where To Look

  • Look at the ring through your left side of the arm (righties) and throught the rght side (lefties)
  • Look and aim for the back two segments of the ring
  • release the ball smoothly and release it high so that it goes in the ring
